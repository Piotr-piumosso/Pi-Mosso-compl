I
Legacy
Corporate Residency
Investment from € 18 000 per season

Annual contract available at € 32 000. One residency per industry vertical, per city.

Request a conversation

Your competitors hold networking dinners. You hold cultural events that your clients request invitations to — years after the fact.

What you are acquiring

  • Six exclusive performances per season — one per month, or structured around your corporate calendar, client dinners, and leadership events
  • Full artistic exclusivity within your industry segment in the contracted city: we do not perform for direct competitors during the partnership
  • Bespoke programme design for each event — aligned with your brand identity, occasion, and the specific acoustic of your space
  • Two original arrangements composed by Piotr Górski per year, built around your institution's character — pieces that exist only in your context
  • Professional audio recordings of each performance — archive-quality, delivered for your internal communications and event documentation
  • Artist biography and press materials updated for your institution — for invitations, programme notes, and communications with your network

There is a particular tier of client relationship that cannot be built through hospitality alone. What moves people — what generates genuine loyalty and the kind of word-of-mouth that cannot be bought — is the sense of being included in something that was chosen with care, not procured from a catalogue.

A Legacy Residency reframes what your institution offers those evenings. It is not background music. It is the reason the evening is worth attending. Your clients leave having experienced something precise and considered — and they associate that precision with you.

The most durable brand asset a private institution can hold is the belief, among those who matter, that it has taste. This residency is designed to establish exactly that.

"The measure of a private space is what it allows you to feel — and what it gives you to remember."

Piotr Górski · Più Mosso

II
Tableau
Private Estate Concert
Investment from € 22 000 per event

Available across Central Europe, DACH, and by arrangement internationally.

Request a conversation

Your residence has always been exceptional. A private concert makes it legendary in the memory of everyone who was present that evening.

What you are acquiring

  • One exclusive concert designed for and performed at your private property — residence, estate, alpine chalet, or venue of your choice
  • Full acoustic consultation prior to the event: the programme is built around the specific resonance, dimensions, and character of your space, not transposed from a concert hall
  • Complete logistics managed by the duo — travel, accommodation, instrument arrangements, and setup coordination — so the evening requires nothing from you except your presence
  • Professional audio recording of the performance, delivered in archival format
  • Thirty numbered copies of a vinyl record: the recording of your concert, pressed with your name, property, and date. Each copy hand-signed by Piotr Górski and Zofia Andersz
  • Twelve-month programme exclusivity: the specific programme performed at your event will not be performed elsewhere for one year
  • Programme notes authored by Piotr Górski, printed and delivered ahead of the concert — for your guests

There is a specific quality of evening that happens when the music was chosen for this room, for these people, for this occasion — and everyone present can feel that it was. Not because of the technical precision of the performance, but because of the intention behind every decision: the repertoire, the sequence, the dynamics, the length of the silences between pieces.

The vinyl record is the physical evidence that the evening happened. Thirty copies, numbered. Given to whom you choose. Each one carries the date, the place, your name. A year from now, the people who received one will have something on their shelf that cannot be replicated.

The value of the Tableau engagement is not measured in hours of music. It is measured in the number of conversations your guests will begin with the words: "I was there when — "

"Every collector of fine art understands that value exists beyond what can be sold. A commissioned composition is the only work that plays itself."

Piotr Górski · Più Mosso

III
Monolith
Commissioned Composition
Investment from € 45 000

Duration: eight to fifteen minutes. Full transfer of copyright and artistic ownership.

Request a conversation

A commissioned musical composition is the rarest kind of acquisition: an asset that requires performance to exist, and in that performance, your taste becomes audible to the world.

What you are acquiring

  • An original composition by Piotr Górski — eight to fifteen minutes for violin and organ or piano — developed over three months with as much or as little input from you as you wish to give
  • Full transfer of copyright and ownership: the original handwritten manuscript, the engraved score, all digital files, and all associated rights transfer to you upon delivery
  • World premiere performance by Piotr Górski and Zofia Andersz at a venue of your choice — private, or public if you wish to mark the occasion formally
  • Studio recording of the world premiere, mastered to archival quality, delivered in both lossless digital and physical formats
  • Certificate of artistic provenance, signed by Piotr Górski, countersigned by a notary — documenting the creation, the commission, and the transfer of ownership
  • Naming rights: the piece may bear any title you choose, including a dedication, a place name, or no name at all
  • All future public performances of the work — by any ensemble — require your authorisation. You may license, restrict, or place the work into permanent privacy

Every significant collection tells a story about the judgment of the person who built it. A painting, a sculpture, a rare manuscript — each of these exists independently of whoever owns it. A commissioned composition does not. Until it is performed, it has no presence in the world. The decision to commission it, to have it played, to give it a name — these are authorial acts, and they belong to you.

The Monolith is not an entertainment product. It is the creation of an asset that increases in significance over time, as Piotr Górski's career develops and the rarity of early commissions becomes part of the work's documented history. What you purchase now is a piece that did not exist before you asked for it. What you hold afterward is something that exists because you chose to.

Unlike any other asset in a private collection, this one can be heard without entering a room — and whoever hears it will know who it belongs to.

How to proceed

Begin with a conversation, not a form.

Each of these engagements begins with a direct conversation between us. There is no application process, no call-for-submissions, no standard contract. We take on three to four engagements of this nature per year. If the timing and the context are right, we will know within the first exchange.
