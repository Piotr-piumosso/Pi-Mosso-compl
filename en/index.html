About us

Violin and organ. We have played together for years.

Every piece has its shape and its place in the programme.

Weddings, churches, concert halls, festivals, cultural centres. The programme follows the acoustics and character of the space — not a ready-made list.

We take a ceremony seriously. We know when to start, when to stop, when to stay quiet.

You write, we answer. Details sorted without long back-and-forth.

Zofia Andersz · Violin

Plays with ease, precision and a feel for the space.

She studies with Prof. Dr. habil. Dorota Kwiecińska. She performs at chamber music festivals, orchestral projects and solo concerts. Her repertoire spans classical music, film music and entertainment. The same awareness of sound every time.

Piotr Górski · Organ and Piano

Prize-winner. Graduate with distinction.

Prize-winner at international organ competitions, graduate of the Poznań Academy of Music with distinction. He performs regularly and looks for projects where classical preparation meets interesting spaces or unusual programmes.
